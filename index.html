Evaluation Rubric

Use the rubric below to score your prompt. Each criterion ranges from 1 (below basic) to 4 (advanced).

# Criterion 4‑point descriptions RRL backing
1 Clarity of Prompt (Explicitness) 4 – fully explicit; … 1 – very vague Jiang et al. 2023; Reynolds & McDonell 2021
2 Specificity / Detail of Information 4 – exact details; … 1 – no details Lee et al. 2022; Benotti & Cusack 2023
3 Use of Prompt‑Engineering Strategies 4 – multiple strategies; … 1 – none Shin et al. 2024; Jiang et al. 2023
4 Alignment with Objectives / Academic Accuracy 4 – directly addresses objective; … 1 – misaligned Jones & Hindle 2023; Lee & Bonner 2022
5 Output Structure / Format Guidance 4 – explicit structure; … 1 – none Shin et al. 2024; Zhou & Pan 2023
6 Cognitive Demand / Higher‑Order Thinking 4 – analysis/synthesis/evaluation; … 1 – factual recall Anderson & Krathwohl 2001; Jones & Hindle 2023
